Suicide Rates Overview: 2001 to 2015

Compares socio-economic info with suicide rates by year and country.

This compiled dataset was pulled from four other datasets linked by time and place, and was built to find signals correlated to increased suicide rates among different cohorts globally, across the socio-economic spectrum.
